Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I exceed my monthly query limit?
We won't cut you off. You'll receive a notification at 80% and 100% of your limit. After that, requests continue at $0.002/query (Free), $0.001/query (Pro), or $0.0005/query (Scale). You can also set hard limits in your dashboard.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?
Yes! Pro and Scale plans include a 14-day free trial with full access. No credit card required to start. You'll only be charged if you choose to continue after the trial.
Can I self-host instead of using the cloud API?
Absolutely. ParcelData is fully open source (MIT). Clone the repo, bring your own data licenses, and run it on your own infrastructure. The cloud plans are for those who want managed data, infrastructure, and support.
What data sources are included?
Free tier includes public records (county assessor, tax, basic property data). Pro adds MLS listings and commercial data. Scale includes all 13 categories: property records, valuations, MLS, permits, zoning, title & liens, environmental risk, schools & demographics, market analytics, commercial, HOA records, and comparable sales.
Do you offer annual billing?
Yes. Annual billing saves 20%. Pro is $39/mo billed annually ($468/year). Scale is $159/mo billed annually ($1,908/year). Contact us for custom enterprise agreements.
What counts as a "query"?
Each API call or MCP tool invocation counts as one query. Batch requests count as one query per property in the batch. Search queries count as one query regardless of results returned. Detail tier does not affect query count.
