Data Provider Partnerships

We aggregate data from 20+ sources through licensed partnerships and public records access. Our entity resolution pipeline merges these into a single canonical record per property.

County Records

3,000+ county assessor databases, tax records, and deed recordings across all 50 states.

MLS Systems

Licensed access to listing data from major MLS providers covering active, pending, and sold properties.

Permit Databases
